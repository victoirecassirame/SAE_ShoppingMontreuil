Go Shopping in Montreuil

Welcome to all students, we hope you're getting used to France! This page displays a little gathering of some useful informations about where to go shopping in Montreuil.

This page is divided into 3 parts:
Firstly, the introduction that you're now reading.
Secondly, you'll find a little tour we made for you, it includes several types of shops (for school, food, beauty, clothing and so on). Of course, this tour was made so that you can complete it in an afternoon but they are also some nice shops a little further, don't hesitate to ask us about them!
Lower, you'll find useful informations about the shops themselves.

We hope this page provide you the informations that you need and that you will enjoy the tour we propose!

The Tour

Carrefour
Le Bergerac
Nonante
StoryBulle
Beers And Records
Grand Angle

Departure : Rue de l'église (about 3 minutes from Mairie de Montreuil)

  1. The 1st step is a bar called Le Bergerac.
  2. The 2nd one is a grocery shop where you can buy natural products aso.
  3. For out 3rd step, we recommand a book shop called StoryBulle. They have a great books selection and some even are in english which is why we chose to show you this shop! It aims a public from 10 to adult so you will easily find what you're looking for.
  4. Next, we have "Beers and Records", you can already tell from the name that it's a really cool place. Plus, the bar owner is reaally nice, he grew up in the city so he knows good tips.
  5. Following, we have an outside mall with some nice shops of differents types. It's very pleasant to go shopping there with your friends!
  6. Finally, a little less entertaining but necessary : You can go check the Carrefour. It's a big superMarket,really practical.

End of the tour : You are near the line 9 of the metro, at the station "Mairie de montreuil".
